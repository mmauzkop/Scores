Overview

So what is Scores?

"Scores" is a mobile application with the concept of earning discounts by performing specific tasks in a variety of stores. Every month the tasks are renewed in all brands and stores.
The tasks are generated randomly. When the customer completes a task, he will earns stars. Stars are kind of tokens, With the Stars you can "purchase" discounts from a huge list of shops.

02

Main Problem

Why the app came?

Some people are not decisive when it comes to shopping and sometimes it can come at a high expenses on wasted things. People can be addicted to shopping. most of the people are buying clothes or going out to restaurants. knowing that they spend a lot of money.
"Scores" comes to give a kind of gift through every purchase you make on the app and that solves the "frustration" problem from the big expenses.

Questions & Answers

Can people be addicted even more with that app?

No. we sincerely take the disadvantages of addiction to shopping. And so we put pop-up notifications to warn the user from that. User can travels across the webs for 20 mins each day. In addition, Users cannot stay inside the shop's website for 2 mins without doing any actions. The system will kick the user out

How the leaderboard works?

The leaderboard is the best part. Each month, the users can win prizes. The chance to get the first place depends on how much the same user spends from his time to buy through the "Scores". Each week you are competing against 8 players to be the top place Every month the prizes are replaced with new others. And on Holidays and Special are the best chance to wins a great prize. See more

We are talking about an app. that works with money. isn't too risky? Can I trust it?

The profile integrate with your own Email/Google/Facebook or Amazon account. And any passwords are sended with an asymmetric encryption. Also "Scores" works and maintains wuth an highly security system against any malwares or malfunctions that can be distrupt our system.You don't need to worry.

Design

Leaderboard

In Scores, there is a chance to get some prizes by winning the Leaderboard each month. The user competes against 8 random users, they compete on who's the user that spends the most time on Scores. The Leaderboard prizes is refreshed on the beginning of each month.

Histories

The "Histories" is a very useful tool in Scores. You can see all the actions you have made since you signed up for the app. The Histories are divided into 3 categories. The category of Last actions: where you can see the last 8 actions you did. The category of Incomes. where you can see all the "Shop" actions you have made. And The category of Expenses, where you can see all the "Gifts" actions you have made.
